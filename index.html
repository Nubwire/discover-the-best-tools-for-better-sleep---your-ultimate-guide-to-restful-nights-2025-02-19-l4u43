Founded in 2020 by a team of sleep specialists and tech innovators, SleepGuide emerged from a simple observation: despite spending a third of our lives sleeping, most people lack access to professional sleep optimization guidance. Our journey began with a mission to democratize sleep science and make expert sleep advice accessible to everyone.
